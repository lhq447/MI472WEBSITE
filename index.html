About US

EXTREME OFF-ROAD & REPAIR is a family run tuning shop that has been serving off-road enthusiasts in the Lansing area since 1980. Whether you're new to outdoor adventure travel or a seasoned veteran on the beaten track, we can help you gear up. From hanging and body armor to sleeping and storage solutions, we're passionate about the outdoors and adventures exploring them. When helping you choose the right product for you, you can rest assured that we have real, field-tested knowledge for you.
